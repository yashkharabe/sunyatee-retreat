Reviving ancient wisdom to navigate the modern world with grace, clarity, and inner peace.

Sunyatee Retreat

At Sunyatee Retreat, we offer a transformative space for individuals to experience inner stillness, clarity, and self-mastery through the profound practice of Sunya Samatha Vipasyana.

Rooted in ancient meditative traditions and adapted for modern life, our retreats provide a structured approach to mindfulness, self-awareness, and holistic well-being.

Whether you seek stress relief, enhanced focus, emotional healing, or spiritual awakening, Sunyatee Retreat offers a peaceful sanctuary to reconnect with your true self.

Our Vision

We envision a world where individuals experience profound inner peace and clarity through the practice of Sunya Samatha Vipasyana. Rooted in ancient wisdom and guided by the philosophy of Sunya, our retreats empower individuals to cultivate mindfulness, balance, and harmony in their personal and professional lives. We aspire to nurture peace ambassadors who contribute to a more awakened and compassionate society.

Our Mission

To provide immersive residential and non-residential Sunya Samatha Vipasyana retreats that guide practitioners from self-awareness to true liberation. We aim to bridge ancient wisdom with modern living, making spiritual wisdom accessible and relevant through structured meditation, storytelling, and interactive learning.

Our Impact Initiatives

Inner Transformation

We offer immersive retreats that guide practitioners through the seven levels of Sunya practice, enabling them to progress from self-awareness to true liberation through structured meditation and wisdom.

Training Peace Ambassadors

Through the Sunyadoota Program, we train dedicated individuals to become guides and trainers. These peace ambassadors help spread the practice across universities, corporate spaces, and community settings.

Corporate Wellness

Customized programs to address stress, anxiety, and lack of focus in professional environments, empowering individuals to navigate challenges with mindfulness and strategic clarity.

Community Empowerment

Extending the transformative benefits of Sunya practice to women entrepreneurs, self-help groups, and rural communities, fostering self-reliance and collective growth.

Retreat Centre Vision

Establishing a permanent Sunya Retreat Centre as a hub for deep meditation, training, and research in Sunya philosophy and holistic well-being.

Global Digital Reach

Utilizing structured online courses and AI-powered coaching to expand the reach of Sunya practice globally, making it accessible to seekers from all backgrounds.

Bridging Wisdom with Modern Life

Our programs integrate the timeless teachings of the Buddha-Dhamma and the Diamond Sutra into daily life, making spiritual growth achievable in today's fast-paced world.
