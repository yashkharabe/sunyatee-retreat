Our Guide and Mentors

Meet the visionary leaders guiding your spiritual journey

Datuk Dr. Lim Siow Jin

Architect of Inner Harmony and Global Wellness Through the Sunya Practice

Zen Master Datuk Dr. Lim Siow Jin, also known as Dhyanacharya Nagajiva

A visionary in the field of holistic wellness, meditation, and spiritual awakening. As the founder of Sunyatee International Foundation, he has dedicated his life to guiding individuals toward inner peace, self-realization, and well-being through the transformative Sunya Practice. His teachings blend ancient wisdom, Zen philosophy, and modern wellness principles, offering a holistic approach to health and consciousness.

A Legacy of Awakening

In 1993, Dr. Lim founded DXN Global, a pioneering wellness enterprise dedicated to Ganoderma-based health products. Rooted in his vision of global peace and prosperity, DXN has grown to over 180 countries, empowering individuals through sustainable health solutions and economic opportunities. By integrating wellness with financial independence, DXN transforms lives, fostering a healthier, more prosperous world. Dr. Lim’s mission continues to inspire millions, creating a lasting impact on holistic well-being and global harmony.

The Vision of Sunya Practice

Dr. Lim’s Sunya Practice is a transformative meditative discipline that guides individuals into deep stillness and clarity. Centered on the principle of emptiness (Sunya), it helps dissolve ego and negativity, fostering inner peace. Its simplicity makes it accessible to all, promoting mindfulness, resilience, and emotional balance. By reconnecting with their pure essence, practitioners experience profound self-awareness and harmony, integrating Sunya’s wisdom into daily life for lasting personal growth and well-being.

The Approach

Dr. Lim’s philosophy transcends meditation, embracing a holistic lifestyle that integrates nutrition, natural healing, and positive thinking for overall well-being. Drawing from traditional Chinese medicine, Ayurveda, and Zen Buddhism, his teachings offer a timeless path to physical, mental, and spiritual harmony. By blending ancient wisdom with modern needs, Dr. Lim empowers individuals to cultivate balance, resilience, and inner peace, making holistic wellness accessible to all who seek a deeper, more fulfilling life.

A Global Movement

Dr. Lim’s work has touched countless lives, fostering self-awareness, holistic wellness, and universal harmony. His teachings bridge science and spirituality, blending ancient wisdom with modern practice. Through Sunyatee Retreats, he invites seekers to experience the depth of Sunya, unlocking their true potential and inner peace. With unwavering dedication, Zen Master Datuk Dr. Lim Siow Jin envisions a world where peace, health, and wisdom thrive, guiding individuals on the path to ultimate freedom.

Leadership & Initiatives

Under his leadership, Sunyatee International Foundation has initiated transformative programs, including:

  • Sunya Samatha Vipasyana Retreats Conducted worldwide, both residential and non-residential, these retreats provide individuals with the tools to achieve mental clarity and spiritual growth.
  • Sunya Dootas (Ambassadors of Sunya) A dedicated network of teachers trained to spread Sunya across communities, ensuring its benefits reach people from all walks of life.
  • Sutra Printing on Palm Leaf A revival of ancient wisdom, preserving sacred teachings through traditional craftsmanship.
  • Economic Empowerment Projects Initiatives like the meditation kimono production in Siddipet, Telangana, uplift farmers and women from distressed regions by providing sustainable livelihoods and global recognition.

Dr. Rajesh Savera

Architect of Holistic Wellness and Socioeconomic Transformation

Sunya Pravartak' Dr. Rajesh Savera

An esteemed Ayurveda physician, psychotherapist, and life and wellness coach. His pioneering work in the fields of Sunya Samatha Vipasyana and Ancient Sutra practices has garnered widespread recognition and respect. Dr. Savera’s teachings, grounded in timeless wisdom and holistic health principles, aim to harmonize the mind, body, and spirit, leading individuals towards a state of profound inner peace and enlightenment.

The Sunyatee International Foundation

Inspired by the guidance of his mentor, Datuk Dr. Lim Siow Jin, also known as Dhyanacharya Nagajiva, Dr. Savera founded the Sunyatee International Foundation in 2015. The foundation’s mission is to promote global peace and prosperity through a blend of spiritual and socioeconomic initiatives. Key to its approach is the belief that true well-being encompasses both individual health and community development.

Sunya Samatha Vipasyana & Ancient Sutra

At the heart of Dr. Savera’s philosophy is Sunya Samatha Vipasyana, a meditative practice focused on achieving ‘Sunya’—the void or emptiness representing ultimate spiritual liberation. This practice cultivates mindfulness, deep concentration, and non-attachment to thoughts, enabling practitioners to attain profound self-awareness and tranquility. Complementing this is the Ancient Sutra practice, which involves the study and recitation of ancient texts, providing guidance for living a balanced and enlightened life.

The Sunyatee Retreats

The Sunyatee International Foundation conducts highly impactful Sunya Samatha Vipasyana retreats, both residential and non-residential. These retreats have received overwhelmingly positive feedback for their transformative effects on participants. The simplicity and accessibility of the Sunya Technique make it suitable for all age groups, and hundreds of “Sunya Dootas” across India and the world teach these techniques, spreading the message of peace and mindfulness far and wide.

Innovative Initiatives

Among the foundation’s most notable initiatives is the Kimono Project in Siddipet, Telangana. This project empowers local farmers and women from distressed areas by training them in the production of meditation kimonos. These garments, celebrated for their elegance and quality, are exported to various countries, providing sustainable livelihoods and preserving traditional craftsmanship.

Another innovative venture is the Sutra Printing on Palm Leaf initiative. This project revives an ancient art form, making sacred texts accessible while creating economic opportunities for artisans. These manuscripts serve as cultural artifacts and spiritual guides, reinforcing the foundation’s commitment to preserving ancient wisdom.

Legacy of Transformation

Dr. Rajesh Savera’s work is a testament to the power of holistic practices in fostering both personal and communal well-being. His dedication to Sunya Samatha Vipasyana and Ancient Sutra practices has inspired countless individuals to pursue inner peace and spiritual growth. Through the Sunyatee International Foundation, Dr. Savera has demonstrated how ancient wisdom can drive contemporary solutions for socioeconomic empowerment. His mentor, Datuk Dr. Lim Siow Jin, continues to influence and inspire these efforts, ensuring a legacy of peace, prosperity, and harmony for future generations.
